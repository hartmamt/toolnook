What is AI Readiness?

AI readiness refers to how well your website communicates with AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, and other large language models. By providing clear guidance through llms.txt files and AI guidance links, you can improve how these systems understand and represent your site to users.

What is llms.txt?

llms.txt is a standardized file that provides guidance to AI language models about your website. Similar to robots.txt for search engines, it helps AI systems understand your site's content, structure, and preferences.

AI Readiness Resources

            

                

                    
What to Include in llms.txt

                    
    
                        
  • Site Information: Name, description, purpose, audience
    • 
                        
  • Preferred Behavior: How AI should represent your site
    • 
                        
  • Context Information: Language, update date, ownership
    • 
                        
  • Crawling Preferences: AI crawling rules, indexed content
    • 
                    

                

                

                    
Benefits of AI Readiness

                    
    
                        
  • More accurate representation in AI assistants
    • 
                        
  • Control over how AI systems understand your content
    • 
                        
  • Reduced likelihood of hallucinations or misrepresentations
    • 
                        
  • Improved user experience for AI-assisted browsing
    • 
                    

                

                

                    
AI Guidance Implementation

                    
    
                        
  • Create a plain text file named llms.txt in your root directory
    • 
                        
  • Add an AI guidance link in your HTML head section
    • 
                        
  • Keep your llms.txt updated as your site evolves
    • 
                        
  • Include clear guidance on preferred knowledge depth
