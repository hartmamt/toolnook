How to Use Keywords for SEO

Title Tags

Include your primary keyword in the page title, preferably near the beginning. Keep titles under 60 characters.

Meta Descriptions

Use your primary keyword and a secondary keyword in the meta description. Keep it under 160 characters and compelling.

Content

Use your keywords naturally throughout your content. Aim for a keyword density of 1-2% and include variations.

Headings

Include keywords in your H1, H2, and H3 tags, but keep them natural and relevant to the content.

URLs

Create clean URLs that include your primary keyword. Avoid parameters and special characters.

Image Alt Text

Use descriptive alt text that includes relevant keywords when appropriate.
